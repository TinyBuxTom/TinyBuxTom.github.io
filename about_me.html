About Me

Hi, I am Tom van Pelt. An enthusiastic student of Computer Engineering at the Hogeschool Rotterdam. I have been busy with everything that has to do with computers for almost my entire life. From testing with Arduino's to a complete registration system for the RDM. I continue where others stop.
I like to work alone, but can also easily work in a team. Working with Scrum is no problem for me.

In my free time I like to listen to music and watch films and series. I also often do odd jobs around the house and with family and friends. I have done a number of electrical and carpentry jobs, such as building a small electrical network and making my own furniture.

So if you are interested and you are looking for an enthusiastic and motivated intern, please contact me.
