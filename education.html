High School (2014-2020)

My pre-university education (VWO) before I went to college was at the Jacob van Liesveldt High School in Hellevoetsluis. One of the subjects I had there was Computer Science. There I learned HTML, CSS, Powerpoint and MSW Logo. Some other subjects I had were: Mathematics for scientific studies, Physics, English, German and Dutch.

University of Applied Sciences (2020-2024)

At the Rotterdam University of Applied Sciences, I study (Technical) Computer Engineering. I learn all the ins-and-outs about computer hardware and their programming.

I learned to program a container load- and unload simulation in Java and to program a basic Raytracer in C++. I also learned how to program Arduino's, Raspberry Pi's and some other microcontrollers. Some of the other skills I have gained are working in a team, working with Scrum, programming in Python, C, HTML and CSS and making basic electronic circuits and circuit designs.
